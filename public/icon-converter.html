Icon Converter for Office Add-in

This tool converts your SVG logo to PNG files in the required sizes for Office Add-ins.

16x16 Icon

32x32 Icon

80x80 Icon

Instructions:

  1. Click each "Download PNG" button to save the icon files
  2. Save them as icon-16.png, icon-32.png, and icon-80.png
  3. Upload these files to your /public/assets/ folder
  4. Update your manifest.xml to use .png instead of .svg
